Frequently Asked Questions

How does LightUp Crowd work?

LightUp Crowd synchronizes multiple smartphones' flashlights to create coordinated light shows at events. Simply open the app, join a show using a unique code, and your phone's flashlight becomes part of the synchronized performance.

Will LightUp Crowd drain my battery?

LightUp Crowd is designed to be battery-efficient. However, using your phone's flashlight for extended periods will naturally use more battery than normal. We recommend having your phone charged before participating in longer events.

Can I create my own light shows?

Yes! Event organizers can create custom light patterns through our intuitive pattern editor. You can set timings and effects to match your music or event theme.

Does LightUp Crowd work offline?

Yes, LightUp Crowd works through local network connections and can function in areas with limited connectivity.
